DTFSC presents: Breathe

Designed & Brought to YOU, by US


Our class (EXP-0007 Design Thinking For Social Good) has worked all semester to understand the concepts of design thinking and how it can be applied to mental health concerns on our campus.


Join us to take a break from your finals studying to be immersed into breathing exercises and reflection of things that make you happy. Also check out our formal report of mental health concerns at Tufts.

What is Breathe?


“Breathe” serves as a meditative and reflective experience. As you approach the door to our room, we invite you to take a colorful sticky note and respond to our prompt (which may change day-to-day). Then, enter our room. This experience is meant to give you a break to do nothing other than enter our room, find a comfortable place to sit, and breathe along with beautiful animations projected on the walls surrounding you, designed to guide deep, peaceful breaths. We invite you to breathe, to wonder, and to let yourself live in the moment.


We hope that our space serves you not only in the time you spend in it, but beyond, as a reminder of how powerful even just a few minutes of deep, meditative breathing can be as both a common practice and method to relieve stress at any time you need.

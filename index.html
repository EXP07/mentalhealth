About Us

Welcome!

We’re a group of Tufts students in the 2021 Fall ExCollege course “Design Thinking for Social Change"!

Throughout the semester, we have conducted research on mental health situation at Tufts, and we created this website to document our work and provide all the resources available!

Report

We spent the semester conducting research and trying to dig up the root issues regarding mental health at Tufts.

Here, you can view our interactive report to learn about our discoveries and how to apply them to your college life at Tufts

Art Installation

The art installation is part of our effort to make an impact on the mental health scene at Tufts.

It’ll be installed by December 15th at the Tisch Library. Feel free to check it out if you have time!
